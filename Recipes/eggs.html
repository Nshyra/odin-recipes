Air Fryer Eggs

Description

Quick and easy recipe for eggs in the air fryer. Great source of protien and a great and easy way to start the day.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Spray a ramekin dish with non stick cooking spray. Pour in beaten eggs.
  2. set air fryer to 370 degrees(F) and place the ramekin into the air fryer basket.
  3. Air fry for 3 minutes. stir mixture and cook for another 3 minutes. FLuff with fork and season to taste with salt and pepper.
